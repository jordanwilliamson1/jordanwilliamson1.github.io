Jordan Williamson

Postdoc, Charles University in Prague

I am a postdoc in the Department of Algebra at Charles University, Prague, funded by the Primus grant "Categorical Knörrer periodicity, cluster categories and tensor triangulated geometry”, held principally by Sebastian Opper. Prior to this, in 2020-2022, I was working on the grant "Cohen Macaulay rings and their applications in higher algebra and topology" under the supervision of Liran Shaul. Previously, I was a PhD student at the University of Sheffield supervised by John Greenlees and Sarah Whitehouse, where my thesis was entitled "Algebraic models and change of groups for equivariant spectra".
